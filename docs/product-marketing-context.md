# Product Marketing Context

_Last updated: 2026-03-28_

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** An AI assistant that joins your video calls, connects to your tools, and answers questions with real business data, live.
**What it does:** Vernix joins Zoom, Meet, Teams, and Webex calls as a participant. It connects to tools like Slack, Linear, GitHub, or your CRM via integrations, then answers questions and takes action during the call using live data. It also transcribes in real time, generates summaries, extracts action items, and provides searchable meeting history.
**Product category:** AI meeting assistant with tool integrations
**Product type:** SaaS (web app)
**Business model:** Freemium. Free (5 silent meetings/month, 30 min). Pro (€29/mo or €24/mo annual, 14-day free trial via Polar).

## Target Audience

**Target companies:** Remote-first teams, engineering/product orgs, consultancies, agencies, leadership teams, any team where meetings are the primary decision-making channel.
**Decision-makers:** Team leads, engineering managers, product managers, founders, heads of ops.
**Primary use case:** Having an assistant in meetings who knows your business, can pull live data from your tools, answer questions, and take action, without anyone leaving the call.
**Jobs to be done:**

- "I need answers from our project tracker during a call without switching tabs"
- "I need an assistant that can look up customer data while I'm talking to a client"
- "I need meeting notes without writing them myself"
- "I need to find something someone said across multiple calls"
- "I need action items tracked without manually creating tasks"

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** During meetings, people need information from other tools (project status, customer data, previous decisions) but nobody wants to stop the conversation to look it up. After meetings, knowledge is trapped in people's heads.
**Why alternatives fall short:**

- Otter.ai / Fireflies: transcription-only, no live Q&A, no tool integrations, no cross-meeting search
- Manual notes: split attention, incomplete, biased toward the note-taker
- Microsoft Copilot: Teams-only, no third-party tool integrations
- Recording playback: too slow to search, nobody rewatches

**What it costs them:** Lost context, repeated discussions, missed action items, wasted time tab-switching to look things up.
**Emotional tension:** Frustration of needing a number and nobody can find it. Guilt of not being prepared. Anxiety about missing commitments made on a call.

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct:** Otter.ai, Fireflies, Grain, transcription-focused, no live agent, no tool integrations
**Secondary:** Manual note-taking, shared docs (Google Docs, Notion), labor-intensive, incomplete
**Indirect:** Microsoft Copilot (Teams only), Google Recorder, platform-locked, no third-party integrations

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**

1. Connects to your tools (Slack, Linear, GitHub, CRM) and pulls live data during calls (unique)
2. Voice agent that answers questions and takes action during meetings
3. Silent mode for text-only participation
4. Cross-meeting semantic search across all calls and documents
5. Knowledge base integration (upload your own docs)
6. No plugins, no extensions, paste a link and go

**Why customers choose us:** Tool integrations and live data during calls is the hook. Voice agent is the experience. Cross-meeting search is the lock-in. Zero-friction setup is the conversion driver.

**Messaging hierarchy:**

1. **Lead:** Connect your tools. Get live answers and action during calls.
2. **Support:** Voice agent answers and acts in real-time.
3. **Foundation:** Transcripts, summaries, action items, search.

## Objections

| Objection                              | Response                                                                                     |
| -------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| "Is it safe to have a bot on my call?" | Vernix doesn't store audio/video, only text transcripts. All data is scoped to your account. |
| "Do participants need to consent?"     | Yes. Vernix joins visibly as a named participant.                                            |
| "What about accuracy?"                 | Speaker-identified transcription powered by Recall.ai with AI-enhanced summarization.        |
| "What if I just want transcription?"   | Silent mode, bot joins quietly and responds via text chat only when mentioned.               |
| "What tools does it integrate with?"   | Any tool with an MCP server. Slack, Linear, GitHub, CRM systems, and more.                   |

**Anti-persona:** Teams that rarely have video calls. Companies with strict no-recording policies. Solo users who don't have meetings.

## Switching Dynamics

**Push:** Frustration with tab-switching during calls, manual notes, missed action items, forgotten decisions.
**Pull:** "Paste link and it works" simplicity, live data from tools, automatic summaries, searchable history.
**Habit:** Existing workflows (manual notes, Notion templates, "I'll look it up after the call").
**Anxiety:** "Will it disrupt the meeting?", "Is my data safe?", "Will participants be annoyed?"
