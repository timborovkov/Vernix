Action Items

Every commitment captured, nothing forgotten.

What is an action item?

When someone says "I'll send the report by Friday" or "Let's schedule a follow-up" — that's an action item. A task someone committed to during the meeting.

neurology

AI Detects Them

KiviKova listens for commitments and extracts them automatically.

task_alt

Nothing Gets Lost

Every task gets a person, a deadline, and a status — no manual work.

Action items are the most important output of any meeting. They are the things people committed to doing. But in most meetings, these commitments are spoken and then forgotten. KiviKova catches them automatically: who said they would do what, and by when. After the meeting, you see a clean list with names, deadlines, and status, without anyone having to write anything down.
